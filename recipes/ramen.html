Italian rissoto al fungui, creamy, tasty and delicous !

What we need to cook this recipe:

  • Mushrooms 200g
  • Rissoto arborio 100g
  • Parmesan cheese 40g
  • Butter 20g

    1. Add oilive oil to a pot
    2. Add the rice to the pot and stir
    3. Add vegetable water in to the pot
    4. Keep mixing until creamy
    5. Add butter and cheese and keep stiring
    There you go! You finally have a tasty and creamy authentic italian rissoto!
