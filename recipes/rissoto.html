Risotto al Funghi

Italian risotto al funghi, creamy, tasty, and delicious!

What you need to cook this recipe:

  • Mushrooms 200g
  • Risotto Arborio 100g
  • Parmesan cheese 40g
  • Butter 20g
  1. Add olive oil to a pot.
  2. Add the rice to the pot and stir.
  3. Add vegetable broth to the pot.
  4. Keep mixing until creamy.
  5. Add butter and cheese, and continue stirring.

There you go! You finally have a tasty and creamy authentic Italian risotto!
